A New Stage for Dance in Rome, GA

Our mission is to provide a welcoming, high-quality, and inclusive environment for individuals of all ages and skill levels to learn, grow, and express themselves through the art of dance.

Business Overview

Studio Elevate is a sole proprietorship founded to meet the growing demand for recreational and professional dance education in the Rome, GA community. We will also offer a valuable after-school care and pickup service for working parents, providing a safe and engaging environment that includes dance education and homework assistance. Located in a 3,500 sq. ft. space, the studio will offer a diverse range of dance classes, including ballet, jazz, hip-hop, and adult fitness-oriented classes like Zumba. We will focus on building a strong community and leveraging social media for marketing, targeting families with children and young adults. The initial funding request is to cover build-out costs, equipment, and a six-month operating buffer, with the goal of reaching profitability within the first 18-24 months.

Our Class Offerings

We provide a structured curriculum for children and flexible options for adults, ensuring a high-quality dance education for every student. Our diverse range of classes caters to various interests and skill levels, from foundational movements to pre-professional training.

Children's Classes

  • Creative Movement (ages 3-5)
  • Ballet & Tap Combo (ages 6-8)
  • Hip-Hop (ages 9-12, 13+)
  • Jazz, Lyrical, & Contemporary (ages 9-12, 13+)
  • Pre-Professional and Competition Teams

Adult Classes

  • Adult Ballet and Jazz
  • Zumba and Barre Fitness
  • Social Dance Workshops (Ballroom, Salsa)

After-School Care & Pickup

A new service designed for working parents in the Rome community. We offer reliable pickup from local schools, a safe and structured environment for homework assistance, and a dedicated dance class as part of the daily schedule. This program provides convenience for parents and a positive, enriching after-school experience for children.

Mid-Day Offerings

  • Preschool Dance and Movement: Short, engaging classes for children aged 2-5 to fill the morning hours.
  • Homeschool PE: Structured daytime dance and movement classes to fulfill physical education requirements for local homeschooling families.
  • Adult Fitness & Wellness: Daytime classes like Yoga, Pilates, and Barre to attract a different demographic and monetize the studio's off-peak hours.
